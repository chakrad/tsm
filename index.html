Welcome to The Salt Miners

Found Some Salt? We're Here to Help

We specialize in salt mines, but anywhere salt thrives, we arrive.

The Salt Miners, or TSM for short, was started in 1865, and throughout our lifetime has served to obtain salt from every single corner and edge of our earth.

In our infancy, we were reserved just to any pure sources of salt like mines and bodies of water. But in this new era, we've been able to expand our business to more sectors all across the world due to advances in techologies, the digital world, and entertainment.
